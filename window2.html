221b Baker Street

Explore the famous grounds



The London address, 221b Baker Street, is where you will find the private work of Sherlock Holmes as written about by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.

Mr Holmes shared his place with his loyal friend and colleague Dr John Watson which is were they carried out their client work. Explore the Baker Street area of London and visit 221b Baker Street yourself!
