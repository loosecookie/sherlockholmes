Measuring Mr Holmes

Novels & short stories



Sir Arthur Conan Doyle has written four novels and fifty six short stories based on the famous character of consulting detective Sherlock Holmes and partner-in-crime Dr John Watson.

Read about their adventures across London from the original tales. Check out the top rated in popularity to influence you in what to read first! There is also hidden data and fun facts to interest you if you can find them!
