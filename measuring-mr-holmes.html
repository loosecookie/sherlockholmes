Measuring Mr Holmes

Dive into one of the world’s famous storylines!

Click on the books to grasp an insight of their adventures!

No.1 Rated Novel

The Hound of the Baskervilles

Sherlock Holmes and Dr Watson investigate the legend of a supernatural hound, a beast that may be stalking a young heir on the fog-shrouded moorland that makes up his estate.

No.2 Rated Novel

The Sign of Four

Miss Mary Morstan request help with the mystery of her missing father, her anonymous gifts of pearls and a letter requesting her to meet an unknown person that evening.

No.3 Rated Novel

A Study in Scarlet

Holmes is summoned to the scene of a baffling murder of an American, inviting along his new companion Dr Watson. Unravelling a tangled thread of murder that extends back 25 years.

No.4 Rated Novel

The Valley of Fear

Holmes gets a message from one of Moriarty’s agents. Despite the fact the law sees him as innocent, Sherlock firmly believes him to be the puppet master of a criminal underworld.

No.1 Rated Short Story

The Speckled Band

Two twin girls raised but their violently tempered widowed father due to the death of his wife in a railway incident. As the story progresses, one of the twins mysteriously die.

No.2 Rated Short Story

The Red Headed League

A man was left curious because of a help wanted advertisement he found in the newspapers offering jobs to strictly red headed people.

No.3 Rated Short Story

A Scandal in Bohemia

The King of Bohemia plans to marry a Scandinavian princess, but first needs to destroy evidence of a previous relationship he had with Irene Adler.

No.4 Rated Short Story

Silver Blaze

A murder occurs and the champion racehorse, Silver Blaze, had disappeared. Sherlock believes the wrong person has been convicted.

No.5 Rated Short Story

The Blue Carbuncle

At 4 o’ clock on Christmas, a tall man is seen getting into an altercation with a few hooligans. The man ends up dropping his hat and a dead goose, but who is the man?

No.6 Rated Short Story

The Musgrave Ritual

The disappearance of two servants, but more excitingly, looking into the meaning of the Musgrave Ritual; a recital of seemingly meaningless phrases.

No.7 Rated Short Story

The Final Problem

An encounter with Moriarty, shows Holmes’s willingness to sacrifice himself for the greater good, and Watson willingly to stand alongside his friend in most dangerous times.

No.8 Rated Short Story

The Empty House

After what was thought to be the death of Sherlock Holmes, his survival arose with the unsolved case of the death of Ronald Adair, a man shot inside a locked room.

No.9 Rated Short Story

The Dancing Men

Holmes has to decipher the code hidden in what appears to be a child’s drawing that frightens a grown woman half to death.

No.10 Rated Short Story

The Six Napoleons

A tale that hints of some mania to do with Napoleon Bonaparte; for someone is going around destroying the busts of the famous Frenchman, and even committing burglary.

No.11 Rated Short Story

The Bruce-Partington Plans

Some sensitive plans for a new submarine are stolen. The suspected thief was discovered dead on some train tracks with all but the most important three pages of the plans.

No.12 Rated Short Story

The Man with the Twisted Lip

Watson finds Holmes is in disguise at an opium den, investigating the disappearance and possible murder of a man.
